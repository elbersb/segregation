# segregation: Entropy-based segregation indices

Calculate and decompose entropy-based, multigroup segregation indices,
with a focus on the Mutual Information Index (M) and Theil's Information
Index (H). Provides tools to decompose the measures by groups and units,
and by within and between terms. Includes standard error estimation by
bootstrapping.
